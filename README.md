## Why Logic Lift?
Most exam aspirants practice from PDFs, random websites, or Telegram channels.
Logic Lift brings:
- Structured problem sets (e.g. SSC 50, ACCA 50)
- Difficulty-based progression
- Clean, distraction-free practice
- Eventually: analytics, AI hints, and community solutions

## Who is it for?
- Competitive exam aspirants (SSC, Banking, etc.)
- Professional certification candidates (ACCA, CPA, etc.)
- Anyone who wants logic-first exam prep
